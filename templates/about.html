About the Conference

Conflan is an annual international conference focusing on innovative research across multiple fields of computer science, engineering, and applied mathematics.

Objectives

  • Bring together researchers, professionals, and students to share their latest findings.
  • Facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration.
  • Support early-career researchers with a platform to present their work.

Conference Topics

The conference accepts papers on, but not limited to:

  • Machine Learning and AI
  • Optimization and Operations Research
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science and Big Data
  • Cybersecurity and Networks

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: July 15, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: August 10, 2025
  • Conference Dates: September 25-27, 2025
